    Mandate

    Mission/Vision statement

    1. To win souls for Christ through a visual/audio encapsulated message. We believe in the power of sight, and that if we can demonstrate scripture through drama, it would go a long way to better lives. We believe our assignment is crucial as the words of a preacher may be forgotten, music may be lost but a combination of picture and audio, sticks.
    2. To glorify God through the “gift” He has embedded in us.
    3. To take over the mountain of entertainment. Jesus said “go into the world”, we have so many worlds, the worlds of sport, technology, business and entertainment; which is our focus. Many ungodly content is being produced day in and day out from the world of entertainment which is making the work of the preacher less easy.
    4. To evangelize: one of the core assignments of a believer is to evangelize but we choose to evangelize through our plays which are scripturally inclined.

    About Us

    Just as the name “THE MIRROR DRAMA MINISTRY” implies, we are a ministry which is sponsored and led by the Holy Spirit, our senior partner. We are evangelists, pastors, prayer is paramount to us as it is our engine room, so we are a prayer band.
